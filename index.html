About Me

I am a Senior Scientific Officer at the Department of Plant Resources, Ministry of Forests and Environment, Nepal. My research focuses on vegetation ecology, ecosystem modeling, and geospatial analysis of environmental change in the Himalayas.

Research Interests

  • GIS & Remote Sensing Applications
  • Climate Change Modeling
  • Medicinal & Aromatic Plants
  • Ethnobotany & Conservation
